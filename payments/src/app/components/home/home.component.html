Aruna Group of Companies (AGC) has established themselves as a leading manufacturer and supplier of black tea for direct exporters in the domestic market under the name of Aruna Tea Factory (Pvt) Ltd in Kaluthara District, Sri Lanka and was incorporated in the year 2004 under the Companies Act of 1982. The total factory area is sq.ft. 70,000 which is located on an extent of 5acres in the Meegahatanna, Kumbaduwaa Estate.
